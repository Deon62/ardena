About Oparides

Kenya's leading peer-to-peer car rental platform

Our Mission

Oparides is Kenya's leading peer-to-peer car rental platform, dedicated to connecting verified car owners with renters across the country. We believe in making car rental accessible, safe, and convenient for everyone.

What We Do

We provide a comprehensive platform that enables:

  • Car Owners: List and manage their vehicles for rent, reaching a wide audience of potential renters
  • Renters: Browse and rent from a diverse selection of verified vehicles from trusted owners
  • Drivers: Offer professional driving services to users who need chauffeur assistance
  • Service Providers: Connect with customers seeking car detailing, roadside assistance, moving services, and more

Our Platform Features

Verified Users

All car owners and renters go through a verification process to ensure safety and trust.

Secure Payments

Multiple secure payment options including M-Pesa, Visa, and Mastercard with encrypted transactions.

24/7 Support

Round-the-clock customer support to assist with bookings, inquiries, and any issues.

Premium Vehicles

Access to a wide range of vehicles from economy to luxury, all maintained to high standards.

Destination Discovery

Discover amazing destinations, events, and automotive businesses across Kenya.

Professional Services

Access to chauffeurs, car detailing, roadside assistance, moving services, and more.

Our Commitment

At Oparides, we are committed to:

  • Providing a safe and secure platform for all users
  • Ensuring all vehicles meet safety and quality standards
  • Offering transparent pricing and clear terms
  • Delivering exceptional customer service
  • Supporting the growth of the sharing economy in Kenya
  • Promoting responsible and sustainable transportation
