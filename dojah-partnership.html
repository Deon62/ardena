Why verification matters on ardena

When you book a car from someone you have never met — or lend your vehicle to a stranger — the only thing standing between trust and risk is identity. ardena solved this by partnering with Dojah, Africa's leading KYC infrastructure provider, to verify every person on the platform before they can rent or list a vehicle.

The result: a closed community where everyone has passed the same identity check that Kenya's banks and fintechs rely on. Not a phone number. Not an email. A verified government ID, matched to a face.

What renters go through

Before a renter can complete a booking on ardena they go through Dojah's identity check:

  1. Government ID submission — national ID, passport, or driver's licence photographed through the app.
  2. Database authentication — Dojah checks the document against official government records and detects tampering.
  3. Facial match — a selfie is biometrically matched to the ID photo.
  4. Liveness detection — confirms a real person is present, not a photo or video.

Once verified, the renter receives a Verified status visible to hosts. Hosts can see who is requesting their vehicle before they approve a booking.

What this means when you rent

Every time you book through ardena you benefit from the fact that the host has also been through the same Dojah KYC process. You are not dealing with an anonymous listing. You know:

  • The host's government ID has been authenticated
  • Their face has been matched to that document
  • ardena holds a verified record tied to any dispute or claim
  • The vehicle listing is linked to a real, accountable person

This protects you legally and practically — and it is the same standard of identity assurance used by Kenya's licensed financial institutions.

Building Kenya's car hire trust ecosystem

Kenya's peer-to-peer car hire market has historically had no standard for identity verification. Ghost listings, unverifiable hosts, and anonymous renters created risk on both sides of every transaction.

The ardena × Dojah partnership changes that. By requiring KYC verification from everyone — renters and hosts alike — ardena creates a marketplace where accountability is built in, not bolted on. Every verified profile is a signal that this platform operates at the same trust level as the country's regulated financial services.

Frequently asked questions

Does ardena verify renters before they book?
Yes. Renters complete a Dojah government ID check and facial match through the ardena app before they can make a confirmed booking.

What is Dojah?
Dojah is an African identity infrastructure company providing KYC and verification services to banks, fintechs, logistics companies, and platforms across the continent. Learn more at dojah.io.

Is my ID data stored by ardena?
No. KYC data is processed and held by Dojah under strict data protection policies. ardena does not store raw ID documents. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What if my verification fails?
Dojah provides a reason if verification cannot complete automatically. Common issues — lighting on the ID photo, an expired document, or a selfie capture problem — are easily resolved. Our team reviews any exception and responds within 24 hours.

Does verification affect how quickly I can book?
Most verifications through Dojah complete within minutes. Once your Verified status is active it applies to all future bookings — you only go through the process once.
