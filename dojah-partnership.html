Identity-verified by design

Every host on ardena is verified through Dojah — Kenya's leading KYC infrastructure — so your booking is always backed by a real, checked identity.

Why ardena partnered with Dojah

Trust is the foundation of any car rental marketplace. When you book a vehicle on ardena you are handing money to someone you have likely never met. We wanted to eliminate any doubt about who that person is.

Dojah provides enterprise-grade KYC infrastructure used by banks, fintechs, and mobility platforms across Africa. By integrating Dojah into our host onboarding flow, ardena ensures that every person who lists a vehicle has had their government-issued ID authenticated, their face matched to that document, and their liveness confirmed — all before a single vehicle goes live on the platform.

How host KYC verification works

When a new host applies to list on ardena the following steps happen automatically through Dojah:

  1. ID document submission — The host photographs their government-issued ID (national ID, passport, or driver's licence).
  2. Document authentication — Dojah checks the document against official government databases and detects tampering or forgery.
  3. Facial recognition match — A selfie is captured and matched against the ID photo using Dojah's biometric engine.
  4. Liveness detection — The system confirms a real person is present, ruling out photo or video spoofing.
  5. ardena review — Our team receives a verification result. Approved hosts receive a Verified Host status and can list vehicles. Any exceptions are reviewed manually before approval.

What this means for renters

When you open the ardena app and see a vehicle listing with a Verified Host badge, it means:

  • The host's identity has been confirmed against a government-issued document
  • Their face has been biometrically matched to that document
  • ardena holds a verified record of who listed the vehicle
  • Any dispute or claim has a verified identity attached to it — protecting you legally

You are not renting from an anonymous profile. You are renting from a person whose identity has been checked by the same technology trusted by Kenya's banks.

What this means for hosts

Completing Dojah KYC verification through ardena gives you:

  • A Verified Host badge that builds renter confidence in your listings
  • Faster booking approvals — renters trust verified profiles more
  • Protection in disputes — your verified identity anchors any claim you make
  • Eligibility for insurance coverage on rentals through ardena

Verification is done entirely through the ardena host app. Most hosts are approved within minutes. You will be notified by push notification and email when your verification is complete.

Building a trust ecosystem in Kenya

Kenya's car hire market has historically struggled with fraud, ghost listings, and unverifiable hosts. ardena and Dojah are working to change that by introducing a layer of verifiable trust that did not exist before in peer-to-peer car rental in Kenya.

This partnership is part of ardena's broader commitment to making car access in Kenya safer, more reliable, and more transparent — for renters, for hosts, and for the communities they serve. Every verified host is a signal to the market that professional, trustworthy peer-to-peer car hire is not just possible in Kenya — it is here.

Frequently asked questions

Is my ID data shared with third parties?
KYC data is processed by Dojah under strict data protection policies. ardena does not store raw ID documents. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What if my verification fails?
Dojah provides a reason if verification cannot be completed automatically. Our team reviews these cases and will contact you by email within 24 hours. Common issues are easily resolved — poor lighting on the ID photo, expired documents, or a face-capture issue.

Does KYC affect renters as well?
Currently the mandatory KYC requirement applies to hosts listing vehicles. Renter identity checks apply at the point of booking for certain vehicle categories. This is evolving as the platform grows.

Who is Dojah?
Dojah is an African identity infrastructure company providing KYC, AML, and data services to banks, fintechs, logistics companies, and platforms across the continent. Learn more at dojah.io.
