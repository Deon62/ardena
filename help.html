Help

Answers and support for ardena car rental in Kenya

Getting started

ardena connects renters with verified vehicle owners across Kenya. Download the ardena client app to browse, book, and drive. Create an account, choose your dates, pick a car, and pay securely through the app. You can also join internal app testing to try the beta.

Renting a car

In the ardena client app you can:

  • Browse available vehicles and filter by location, dates, and price
  • View vehicle details, photos, and host profiles
  • Book and pay with M-Pesa, Visa, or Mastercard
  • Coordinate pickup and return directly with the host

Download the app from the Applications page.

Payments

We accept M-Pesa, Visa, and Mastercard. Payments are processed securely in the app.

If something goes wrong

Our support team is available 24/7. Report issues through the app or contact us directly. For accidents, contact the police first, then report to ardena within 24 hours with a police abstract for insurance claims.

Getting started as a host

Turn your idle vehicle into income. Download the ardena host app, create an account, verify your identity, and list your car. Set your availability and price‚Äîwe suggest ranges based on your vehicle and location.

Listing your car

In the host app you upload photos, add vehicle details (make, model, year), and submit registration and insurance. Once verified, your listing goes live. See the Host page for full steps and requirements.

Finances & earnings

You set your own rates. ardena charges a 15% service fee on each completed booking. You receive earnings within 48 hours after each rental via M-Pesa or bank transfer.

Payments

Payments from renters are processed securely in the app. Hosts receive payouts within 48 hours of rental completion.
