How to Host Your Car

Getting started is simple. List your vehicle in a few steps and start earning—whether you're side-hustling or building a gig. We keep it clear and flexible.

02

Finances & Earnings

Understand how you'll earn money and manage your finances as a host on ardena.

Set Your Own Rates

You control your pricing. Set competitive rates based on your vehicle type, location, and market demand. Adjust prices anytime through the app.

Service Fee

ardena charges a 15% service fee on each completed booking. This covers platform maintenance, insurance, customer support, and payment processing.

Fast Payouts

Receive your earnings within 48 hours after each rental completion. Payments are processed securely via M-Pesa or bank transfer directly to your account.

Income Potential

Earn anywhere from KSh 5,000 to KSh 50,000+ per day depending on your vehicle. Active hosts typically earn 30-50% of their car's value annually.

What Affects Your Earnings?

Vehicle type and condition: Luxury and well-maintained cars command higher rates

Location: Urban areas with high demand see more bookings

Availability: More available days mean more earning opportunities

Pricing strategy: Competitive pricing attracts more renters

Managing Bookings

Learn how to effectively manage your bookings and ensure smooth rental experiences.

Booking Requests

Receive booking requests through the app. You have 24 hours to accept or decline each request. Quick responses improve your host rating.

Communication

Communicate directly with renters through the in-app messaging system. Coordinate pickup times, locations, and any special arrangements.

Vehicle Inspection

Conduct a thorough inspection before and after each rental. Document any existing damage and report new damage immediately through the app.

Handover Process

Meet renters at the agreed location and time. Verify their identity, explain vehicle features, and ensure they understand rental terms and conditions.

Cancellations

You can cancel bookings if necessary, but frequent cancellations may affect your host status. Emergency cancellations are allowed with proper documentation.

Reviews & Ratings

After each rental, both you and the renter can leave reviews. Maintain high ratings by being responsive, professional, and maintaining a clean vehicle.

Tips for Successful Hosting

Simple practices that help you attract more renters and earn consistently.

Vehicle Maintenance

Keep your vehicle clean and well-maintained at all times. A well-cared-for car earns better reviews and higher ratings.

Be Responsive

Respond to booking requests promptly. Be flexible with pickup and drop-off times when possible—renters appreciate it.

Clear Instructions

Provide clear instructions about your vehicle's features, fuel policy, and any quirks. It reduces confusion and builds trust.

Build Repeat Business

Build positive relationships with repeat renters. Update your calendar regularly to reflect actual availability.
