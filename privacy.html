Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Introduction

At ardena, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains in detail how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our car rental platform in Kenya. This policy applies to all users of our services, including renters (clients) and vehicle owners (hosts).

This comprehensive privacy policy covers all aspects of data processing on our platform, including account creation, verification processes, booking transactions, communication, and ongoing service delivery. By using ardena, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

3. How We Use Renter Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Creating and managing your rental account
  • Processing rental bookings and payments
  • Providing location-based vehicle recommendations
  • Facilitating communication with vehicle hosts
  • Managing rental transactions and documentation

3.2 Identity Verification and Safety

  • Verifying your identity through official documents
  • Confirming driver's license validity and authenticity
  • Conducting background checks for platform safety
  • Preventing fraud and unauthorized account usage
  • Ensuring compliance with Kenyan transportation regulations

3.3 Communication and Support

  • Sending booking confirmations and reminders
  • Providing customer support and service updates
  • Facilitating secure messaging with hosts
  • Sending important safety and policy notifications
  • Handling emergency communications via secondary contacts

3.4 Platform Improvement and Personalization

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve service quality
  • Personalizing vehicle recommendations based on preferences
  • Developing new features and services
  • Conducting research to enhance user experience
  • Optimizing platform performance and reliability

3.5 Legal and Regulatory Compliance

  • Complying with Kenyan data protection laws
  • Fulfilling tax reporting obligations using KRA PIN
  • Responding to legal requests and court orders
  • Cooperating with law enforcement agencies
  • Maintaining records as required by transportation authorities

5. Data Security and Protection

5.1 Technical Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure Storage: Personal information stored in secure, access-controlled databases
  • Regular Security Audits: Periodic assessments of security measures
  • Firewall Protection: Network security to prevent unauthorized access
  • Intrusion Detection: Monitoring for suspicious activities

5.2 Organizational Security Measures

  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access personal data
  • Employee Training: Regular privacy and security training for staff
  • Data Minimization: We collect only necessary information
  • Regular Reviews: Ongoing assessment of data handling practices
  • Incident Response Plan: Procedures for handling security breaches

5.3 Special Handling of Sensitive Data

  • ID Documents: Stored with enhanced security measures
  • Payment Information: Processed through PCI-compliant payment processors
  • Verification Images: Selfie images are not permanently stored after verification
  • Location Data: Used only for service provision and not shared unnecessarily

7. Your Rights as a Renter

7.1 Access Rights

  • Request a copy of all personal information we hold about you
  • View your complete profile and transaction history
  • Access verification status and documentation status
  • Review communication logs with hosts

7.2 Correction Rights

  • Update your personal profile information at any time
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Update payment methods and billing information
  • Modify communication preferences and notification settings

7.3 Deletion Rights

  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Remove specific information from your profile
  • Delete payment methods (subject to transaction completion)
  • Withdraw consent for specific data processing activities

7.4 Portability Rights

  • Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Transfer your data to other service providers
  • Export your transaction and booking history

7.5 Objection Rights

  • Object to direct marketing communications
  • Restrict processing of specific data categories
  • Request human review of automated decisions

9. International Data Transfers

Your personal information is primarily stored and processed within Kenya. However, we may transfer data to international service providers for:

  • Cloud storage and processing services
  • Customer support platforms
  • Analytics and marketing services
  • Payment processing services

All international transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards including standard contractual clauses and compliance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or operational needs. When we make changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on our website
  • Update the "Last updated" date
  • Notify active users via email for significant changes
  • Provide advance notice for material changes affecting your rights

Your continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Host Privacy Section

13.1 Information We Collect from Hosts (Vehicle Owners)

When you register as a host and list your vehicle on ardena, we collect comprehensive information to ensure safe and reliable service delivery:

13.1.1 Host Account Information

  • Full Legal Name: As appears on official identification documents
  • Email Address: Primary contact method and account identifier
  • Password: Encrypted credentials for account security
  • Mobile Number: Primary contact for communication and verification
  • Date of Birth: To confirm you meet minimum age requirements (18+)
  • Profile Photograph: For identity verification and renter recognition
  • Account Creation Date and IP Address: For security and fraud prevention

13.1.2 Official Documentation

For compliance with Kenyan regulations and platform security:

  • National ID Number: Kenyan national identification for identity verification
  • KRA PIN: Kenya Revenue Authority PIN for tax compliance and payment processing
  • Vehicle Ownership Documents: Logbook, registration certificate, insurance certificate
  • Driving License: Valid Kenyan driving license details

13.1.3 Vehicle Information

  • Vehicle Make, Model, and Year: Complete vehicle identification
  • Vehicle Registration Number: Official registration details
  • Vehicle Identification Number (VIN): Unique vehicle identifier
  • Vehicle Category: Car, SUV, van, truck, motorcycle classification
  • Transmission Type: Manual or automatic transmission
  • Fuel Type: Petrol, diesel, electric, hybrid
  • Seating Capacity: Number of passengers vehicle can accommodate
  • Vehicle Features: Air conditioning, music system, safety features, etc.
  • Vehicle Condition: Current condition and mileage information
  • Vehicle Photos: Exterior and interior images for renter viewing

13.1.4 Location and Availability

  • Vehicle Location: GPS coordinates and address where vehicle is parked/available
  • Service Area: Geographic areas where you offer vehicle rental
  • Availability Calendar: Dates and times when vehicle is available for rental
  • Pickup/Drop-off Locations: Preferred locations for vehicle handover

13.1.5 Pricing and Payment Information

  • Daily Rental Rates: Pricing per day for different rental periods
  • Security Deposit Amount: Required deposit for vehicle protection
  • Bank Account Details: Account number, bank name, branch for payments
  • M-Pesa Number: For mobile money payments and deposits
  • Payout Preferences: Preferred payment methods and frequency
  • Tax Information: KRA PIN for tax withholding and compliance

13.1.6 Insurance and Safety Information

  • Insurance Policy Details: Insurance provider, policy number, coverage limits
  • Vehicle Inspection Reports: Safety and roadworthiness certificates
  • Emergency Contact Information: Secondary contact for vehicle-related emergencies
  • Safety Features: Vehicle safety equipment and modifications

13.2 How We Use Host Information

13.2.1 Service Provision

  • Creating and managing your host account and vehicle listings
  • Processing rental bookings and payments from renters
  • Providing location-based vehicle visibility to potential renters
  • Facilitating communication between hosts and renters

13.2.2 Payment Processing

13.2.3 Verification and Safety

  • Conducting background checks for platform safety
  • Preventing fraud and unauthorized account usage
  • Ensuring compliance with Kenyan transportation regulations

13.2.4 Platform Improvement

  • Analyzing rental patterns to improve service quality
  • Optimizing vehicle visibility and booking rates
  • Developing new features for host management
  • Conducting research to enhance host experience
  • Providing insights on market trends and pricing

13.2.5 Legal and Regulatory Compliance

  • Complying with Kenyan data protection laws
  • Fulfilling tax reporting obligations using KRA PIN
  • Responding to legal requests and court orders
  • Cooperating with transportation authorities
  • Maintaining records as required by law

13.3.3 Legal and Regulatory Sharing

  • Law enforcement agencies when required by law
  • Regulatory authorities for compliance purposes
  • Court orders and legal proceedings
  • Tax authorities using provided KRA PIN information
  • Transportation regulatory bodies
  • Insurance providers during claims processing

13.4 Host Data Security

13.4.1 Technical Security Measures

  • Encryption: All host data encrypted using industry-standard protocols (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure Storage: Host information stored in secure, access-controlled databases
  • Regular Security Audits: Periodic assessments of security measures
  • Firewall Protection: Network security to prevent unauthorized access
  • Intrusion Detection: Monitoring for suspicious activities

13.4.2 Organizational Security Measures

  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access host data
  • Employee Training: Regular privacy and security training for staff
  • Data Minimization: We collect only necessary host information
  • Regular Reviews: Ongoing assessment of data handling practices
  • Incident Response Plan: Procedures for handling security breaches

13.4.3 Special Handling of Sensitive Host Data

  • Bank Account Information: Stored with enhanced security and encryption
  • Vehicle Documents: Protected with additional security layers
  • Insurance Information: Processed through secure channels
  • Location Data: Used only for service provision and not shared unnecessarily

13.5 Host Data Retention

13.5.1 Retention Periods

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Vehicle Listings: Retained for 5 years after account closure

13.5.2 Account Closure

When you close your host account, we:

  • Delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days
  • Retain necessary data for legal and regulatory compliance
  • Remove your vehicle listings from public view immediately
  • Cancel all active bookings and subscriptions
  • Process final payments and refunds within 14 days

13.6.2 Correction Rights

  • Request your host data in a machine-readable format

13.6.5 Objection Rights

2.1 Account Creation Information

When you create an account as a renter, we collect the following information:

  • Authentication Method: Whether you signed up directly, via Google OAuth, or Apple Sign-In

For identity verification and service delivery, we collect detailed personal information:

  • Gender: For demographic analysis and service improvement

2.3 Official Documentation

For compliance with Kenyan regulations and platform security:

  • National ID Number: Kenyan national identification number for identity verification
  • KRA PIN: Kenya Revenue Authority Personal Identification Number for tax compliance
  • Driver's License Details:

2.6 Payment Information

2.7 Communication Data

  • Message Content: All communications between you and hosts through our platform

2.9 Verification and Security Data

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address for security
  • Login History: Times and locations of account access
