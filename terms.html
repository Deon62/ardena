2. About ardena & the Platform

ardena is a peer-to-peer vehicle rental marketplace operating in Kenya. We provide two distinct mobile applications: the ardena Renter app, which allows individuals to discover, book, and pay for vehicles listed by verified hosts; and the ardena Host app, which allows vehicle owners to list, manage, and earn from their vehicles.

ardena acts solely as a technology intermediary and facilitator. We do not own, operate, maintain, or insure any vehicle listed on the platform. The rental agreement for each trip is formed directly between the host and the renter. ardena's role is to provide the infrastructure, verification, payment processing, and dispute support that enables those transactions to take place safely and efficiently.

Renters bear full financial responsibility for any damage, fines, or loss caused during the rental period that is not covered by the applicable insurance policy. Late returns beyond the agreed time will incur additional charges as stated in the booking summary.

9. Vehicle Standards & Listing Requirements

All vehicles listed on ardena must meet the following minimum standards before approval:

  • Valid inspection certificate (NTSA-recognised) dated within the past 12 months;
  • Valid comprehensive insurance policy naming ardena-facilitated rental use;
  • No outstanding finance or court orders restricting transfer or use;
  • Not reported stolen or used in any ongoing criminal investigation;
  • All exterior and interior photos must be current, clear, and accurately represent the vehicle's condition;
  • Functional seatbelts for all seated positions, functional tyres (including spare), working headlights, and a first-aid kit.

ardena reserves the right to remove any listing that does not meet these standards, has received repeated damage complaints, or has been associated with safety incidents. Hosts may be required to resubmit documentation periodically to maintain active listings.

Bookings are made through the ardena Renter app. A booking is only confirmed once the host accepts the request and the renter receives an in-app confirmation notification. Listing a vehicle or initiating a booking request does not by itself constitute a confirmed reservation.

Prices displayed on the platform are set by hosts and include the daily or hourly rental rate plus any applicable ardena service fee. Additional charges may apply for mileage overages, late returns, damage, cleaning, or toll fees incurred during the trip. All pricing is in Kenyan Shillings (KES).

ardena reserves the right to adjust platform service fees at any time. Hosts will be notified of any changes to fee structures with at least 14 days' notice.

11. Payments, Fees & Payouts

All payments on the ardena platform are processed through our secure in-app payment system. Accepted payment methods include M-Pesa and major debit/credit cards. Cash payments are strictly prohibited and will not be recognised or protected by ardena's dispute resolution process.

Upon completion of a trip, ardena will disburse the host's earnings, net of the platform service fee, to the host's registered M-Pesa or bank account within the period stated in the Host app. ardena deducts its service fee from each transaction before disbursement. The service fee rate is displayed in the Host app and may be updated from time to time.

ardena is not a financial institution. Payment processing is facilitated by licensed third-party payment providers. ardena is not liable for payment delays caused by the user's mobile network operator, bank, or the payment provider.

In the event of a disputed charge, both parties must raise the matter through the in-app dispute function within 48 hours of the trip ending. ardena's decision following a reviewed dispute is final, subject to any applicable legal appeal process.

12. Security Deposit

ardena may require renters to pay a refundable security deposit at the time of booking for certain vehicle categories. The deposit amount is displayed during the booking flow before payment is confirmed. The deposit is held by ardena and will be refunded to the renter within 5–7 business days following the successful completion of the trip, provided:

  • No damage to the vehicle is reported or substantiated;
  • The vehicle is returned on time and in agreed condition;
  • No traffic fines, toll charges, or fuel deficits are outstanding;
  • No policy violations occurred during the trip.

If damage or outstanding charges are established following review, ardena may apply all or part of the deposit toward those costs before issuing any remaining refund.

13. Cancellation & Refund Policy

Renter cancellations: Cancellations made more than 48 hours before the scheduled trip start time are eligible for a full refund of any amounts paid, excluding the platform booking fee which is non-refundable. Cancellations made within 48 hours of the trip start time will forfeit 50% of the rental amount. Cancellations made within 6 hours of the trip start time, or no-shows, will forfeit the full rental amount.

Host cancellations: Hosts who cancel a confirmed booking without valid reason will be subject to a penalty deducted from future payouts. Repeated host cancellations may result in listing suspension. In all cases where a host cancels, the renter receives a full refund including any fees paid.

Force majeure: In cases of natural disaster, government-declared curfews, or other extraordinary events beyond the control of either party, ardena may issue full or partial refunds at its discretion.

Refunds are processed to the original payment method and may take 3–10 business days to appear depending on the payment provider.
